## Backstory:
Gink was born into the esteemed caste society of the Grung, where he was expected to follow in his family’s footsteps and become a respected member of their community. However, as fate would have it, his life took an unexpected turn.

One day, while exploring the banks of a river near his home, Gink stumbled upon something that would change the course of his existence forever - a shiny gold coin. The allure of its golden hue was irresistible to him, and he felt an inexplicable connection to the treasure as if it held secrets and mysteries beyond anything he had ever known.

This became Gink's obsession. Finding the greatest treasure in the realms consumed his waking and sleeping mind. He decided to leave Grung society and become a famous treasure hunter.

Gink’s friends and family were shocked by his decision to leave behind their traditional way of life. They saw it as a betrayal of their values and traditions, and the pressure mounted for him to conform.

However, Gink was resolute in his choice. He had found something that ignited a fire within him, driving him to explore beyond the confines of his society. The thought of becoming a treasure hunter consumed him, fueled by the promise of untold riches, adventure, and potentially uncovering secrets about his people’s history.

Gink left behind everything he knew and loved, embarking on an unprecedented journey for his kind. As he set out to explore the world beyond Grung society, he abandoned his traditional attire, adopting a new identity as a treasure hunter.

...

The leader of a guild, Fin, a cunning and resourceful individual, had been watching Gink from afar with great interest. At first, Fin thought Gink was just another foolish Grung trying to leave his mark on the world by becoming a treasure hunter. However, as he observed Gink’s skills and abilities, Fin began to see potential in the young frog.

Fin noticed that Gink possessed an uncanny ability to sneak, hide, and move silently - traits that would serve him well in the art of thievery. Recognizing this talent, Fin approached Gink with a proposition: join his guild as a thief.

Initially, Gink was hesitant, still grappling with his decision to leave his old life behind. But Fin’s persuasive words and promises of a new family among the guild’s members eventually won him over. Fin saw potential in Gink, not just as a thief but also as someone who could bring a unique set of skills to their organization.

As Gink began to learn from Fin and the other guild members, he discovered that his Grung physiology granted him unexpected advantages. His webbed hands and feet allowed for silent movement within water, and his keen senses made it easier to detect hidden dangers or sniff out valuable information. Fin took a particular interest in Gink’s abilities, recognizing that he could be an invaluable asset to their operations.

Under Fin’s guidance, Gink honed his skills as a thief, using his Grung traits to excel in this new role. Fin saw the potential for Gink to become one of the guild’s most valuable members and offered him a place among their ranks.

Gink has grown fond of Fin and his position within the guild. He is slowly learning about the world outside of Grung society, while Fin and some others teach him Common.

Gink still has his lucky coin to this day, and can be seen flipping it between his webbed fingers, while his gaze is transfixed on the TREASURE!
